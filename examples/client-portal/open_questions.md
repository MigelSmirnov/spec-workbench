# Open Questions

## Status

**Open; intentionally unresolved**

1. What is the lifecycle of an approved estimate, including the point at which
   it becomes immutable and the treatment of later changes?
2. What is the business relationship between an approved estimate and a
   factura, given that they are not the same domain fact?
3. What policy determines when and how an approved estimate is published to
   Client Portal?
4. How are estimate versions created, selected, superseded, retained, and
   addressed?
5. How will existing manual pilot budget data migrate to an approved PresuPro
   snapshot when that contract becomes available?
6. What approval workflow will PresuPro or another owning product eventually
   provide?
