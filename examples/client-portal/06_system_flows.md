# State 4 — System Flows

## Purpose

Trace the important end-to-end product flows after models, rules, and ownership
are stable.

## Status

**Not started**

## Expected outputs

- Actor or trigger for each selected flow.
- Boundary, validation, policy, domain operation, and integration transitions.
- Models crossing each boundary.
- Error ownership and observable success or failure outcomes.
- Produced state or artifacts and any rollback requirements.

No flows or orchestration are proposed in this bootstrap.
